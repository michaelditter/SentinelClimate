## ELEVENLABS VOICE TRAINING NOTES

### Voice Profile: Professional Emergency Coordinator
- **Tone:** Calm, authoritative, reassuring
- **Pace:** Measured and clear, slightly slower than conversational
- **Emphasis:** Key information like codes, timelines, and contact numbers
- **Energy:** Professional urgency without panic

### Key Pronunciation Guidelines
- "Harris County" - Clear enunciation, slight pause between words
- Authorization codes - Spell out clearly with pauses between segments
- Phone extensions - Each digit pronounced distinctly
- Technical terms - Slower pace for "telehealth," "crisis intervention"
- Numbers - "150 plus" not "one-fifty plus," "45 percent" not "forty-five percent"

### Emotional Calibration
- **Opening:** Confident and immediately helpful
- **Verification:** Methodical and thorough
- **Activation:** Decisive and reassuring
- **Closing:** Supportive and professional

### Background Audio
- Subtle emergency operations center ambiance
- Occasional keyboard typing
- Low-level radio chatter in background
- Professional but active environment sound
